## Number of tests generated ##

There are 96 unique bugs in total, of which the 189 patches were generated by various repair tools. Of these bugs, DiffTGen and Randoop were able to generate 61 and 956 test cases, respectively.

## Characteristics of patches ##

* Of 189 patches, there are 16 machine-generated patches that are syntactically identical to human patches.
* 187 out of 189 patches have definitive labels by majority voting.
* 2 patches whose labels cannot be decided via majority voting are patches with IDs 117 and 201. The ratings for these two patches are: 2/1/2 and 1/2/2 (unknown/correct/incorrect).
